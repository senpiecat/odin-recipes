Bento

Description

A bento is the Japanese iteration of a single-portion take-out or home-packed meal, often for lunch. Outside Japan, it is common in other East and Southeast Asian culinary styles, especially within Chinese, Korean, Singaporean cuisines and more, as rice is a common staple food in the region.

What to use in your bento

  • Tamagoyaki
  • Rice balls
  • Inari sushi
  • Teriyaki chicken
  • Apple rabbits
  • Soy sauce eggs
  • Tsukune
  • Chicken katsu
  • Hot dog octopus
  • Potato korokke
